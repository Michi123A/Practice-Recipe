Uncle Brent's Hot Fudge

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 2/3 Cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1/2 Cup Flour
  • 2 Cups Hot Water
  • 2 Tablespoons Corn Syrup
  • 4 Tablespoons Butter
  • 2 Teaspoons Vanilla
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt

Directions

  1. In saucepan mix together sugar, salt, cocoa and flour. Making sure there are no lumps
  2. Add corn syrup and hot water.
  3. Bring to full boil. Stirring constantly.
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. Add butter and vanilla, stirring until smooth.
  6. Enjoy over icecream or by itself.

Chocolate-Chip Zuchinni Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups Flour
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 1 Cup (2 Sticks) Melted Butter
  • 2 Tablespoons Milk
  • 1 Tablespoon Vanilla
  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 1/4 Cups Shredded Zucchini
  • 1 Bag Semi-Sweet Chocolate-Chips

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven 325 Degrees
  2. Combine melted butter and sugars until smooth.
  3. Add salt, vanilla and milk.
  4. Add eggs and mix well.
  5. In separate bowl combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and cinnamon.
  6. Add dry ingredients to wet.
  7. Fold in zucchini.
  8. Add chocolate-chips.
  9. Pour into greased baking dish.
  10. Bake 45-55 minutes.
  11. Let cool and enjoy
